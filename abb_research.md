## The engineering model that makes this credible

The cornerstone should be a single **asset and signal graph**. Do not let screens be the primary artifact. The primary artifact should be a machine-readable model containing plant, area, unit, module, equipment, device, and signal nodes, plus relationships such as `feeds`, `drains`, `controlled_by`, `measured_by`, `sister_signal`, `backup_measurement`, and `mass_balance_group`. OPC UA is well suited to this because it already provides hierarchical address spaces, subscriptions, events, methods, access rights, and an extensible object-oriented information model. Once the graph exists, screen generation becomes deterministic rather than magical.
