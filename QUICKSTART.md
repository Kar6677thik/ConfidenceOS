# ConfidenceOS Judge Quick Start

1. Open Fleet Overview to see which plant has the highest risk score.
2. Click the highest-risk plant to open the Operator dashboard.
3. Select any degraded sensor to inspect confidence reasons and health history.
4. Ask ConfidenceOS a question in the right-side query panel.
5. Open Forensics and press Play on the Texas City Incident replay.
6. Open Compliance, generate a report, and download the PDF artifact.
7. Switch roles in the top navigation to compare Operator, Engineer, Manager, and Auditor views.

Demo note: the simulator data is synthetic, but the intelligence layer uses the same data contracts as a real plant feed.
